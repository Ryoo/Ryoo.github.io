About Me

The world around me always fascinates me. Fortunately, my curiosity led me to chemistry. Chemistry taught me about the universe, which filled me with strong feelings of awe and wonder. Of course there is still more to learn, and I'm still in the process of uncovering more beauties in the universe.

I also noticed that there is a large separation between public and science. I really want to share the beauty of the universe from a science perspective. I believe in having child-like wonder that will lead us to a bright future.
